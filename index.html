About Foody-Hotpot & Buffet

"Founded in 2014, Foody-Hotpot & Buffet is the brainchild of Li Chen, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for bringing people together through exceptional dining experiences. Inspired by the rich culinary traditions of hotpot, we embarked on a journey to redefine the hotpot experience in Sichuan, China."

Foody, where the art of hotpot meets a delightful buffet experience. At Foody, we believe in creating more than just a meal; we are on a mission to provide a sensory journey that sparks joy, connection, and culinary adventure.

"Our dedicated team at Foody is passionate about delivering impeccable service and creating a warm, inviting atmosphere. From our chefs crafting flavorful broths to our attentive staff ensuring your dining experience is seamless, each member of the Foody family plays a crucial role in bringing our vision to life."
